About Me

Ever since I laid my eyes upon the nature, my gaze has been transfixed by its tantalizing complexity and simple beauty. I aspire to address the challenges of research in diseases caused by dysregulation of the systems. But how? My answer is the integration of machine learning, deep nerual network and mathematical modeling.
I see deep learning and computational modeling as the most blood-tingling technique that can push the boundary of biomedical research toward a new horizon. Analyzing complex multi-omics datasets will be the next blood test in healthcare.
